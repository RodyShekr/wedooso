E-Commerce SEO: Is SEO Important for eCommerce?

In the ever-evolving digital landscape, the significance of SEO for e-commerce businesses is no longer a topic for debate but an undeniable truth. Search engine optimization (SEO) has emerged as a tool for e-commerce success, serving as business growth, brand recognition, and ultimately increased sales.

25.12.2023

SEO's Unwavering Importance in E-commerce


The importance of SEO for e-commerce businesses is evident in its ability to address the core challenges and opportunities that e-commerce entrepreneurs face:

  • Visibility and Reach:

    SEO helps businesses gain visibility in search results, ensuring their websites are seen by a wider audience of potential customers who are actively searching for the products or services they offer.


  • Traffic Generation:

    By ranking higher in search results, e-commerce websites attract more organic traffic, bypassing the high costs associated with paid advertising campaigns. This organic traffic is more valuable as it is based on genuine user interest, leading to higher conversion rates.


  • Brand Recognition:

    As SEO elevates a website's visibility, it also enhances brand recognition, establishing a strong online presence and fostering customer loyalty. This increased brand awareness can lead to repeat purchases and referrals.


  • Cost-Effectiveness:

    SEO offers a long-term, cost-effective approach to marketing e-commerce businesses, as its results are sustainable and incremental. Unlike paid advertising campaigns that require continuous investment, SEO can yield consistent results for a fraction of the cost.


  • Measurable Results:

    SEO is a data-driven discipline, allowing businesses to track their progress, identify areas for improvement, and optimize their strategies accordingly. This data-driven approach ensures that SEO efforts are aligned with business objectives.

The Impact of SEO on Ecommerce Sales

Numerous studies and case studies have consistently demonstrated the transformative impact of SEO on e-commerce sales and overall business growth:

  • A study by Ascend2 revealed that 72% of online shoppers use search engines to find products and services, highlighting the immense power of search engines in driving traffic to e-commerce websites.


  • Another study by BrightEdge found that businesses that invest in SEO experience a 20% increase in organic traffic, a 10% increase in sales, and a 50% improvement in ROI. These statistics underscore the effectiveness of SEO in driving ecommerce success.


  • An analysis by Search Engine Journal found that businesses that rank in the top 3 positions on Google's first page can experience up to a 700% increase in website traffic from organic search. This highlights the significant impact of higher rankings on traffic generation.


  • A study by Moz found that businesses with high-quality backlinks from reputable websites enjoy a 2.5x increase in organic traffic. This underscores the importance of link building in boosting website authority and search engine rankings.

SEO Power for Sustainable Ecommerce Success

Effective SEO is not a one-off endeavor; it entails a plethora of interconnected strategies that must work in line to achieve optimal results. These strategies are broadly classified into five major pillars:

  • Keyword Research and Optimization:

    Conduct extensive keyword research to delve into the depths of consumer search patterns. Determine the relevant search terms that prospective customers use when looking for products or services similar to those offered by your e-commerce shop. Recognize the subtle aspects of consumer search intent, whether it is informational, navigational, or transactional. Optimize your website's content, titles, and meta descriptions to match these targeted keywords, ensuring that your site appears prominently in relevant search results.


  • Technical SEO Optimization:

    Make sure your website's technical foundation is solid and search engine optimized. Prioritize page loading speed to ensure a smooth user experience and lower bounce rates. Address mobile responsiveness in order to capitalize on the growing trend of mobile-based shopping. Optimize the site structure for easier navigation and crawling by search engines.


  • Content Strategy and Creation:

    Create high-quality, engaging content for your target audience. Share content that is informative, relevant, and valuable to their needs, interests, and pain points. Create a content calendar to plan and publish content on a regular basis, keeping your audience engaged and coming back for more. Use storytelling techniques to captivate and emotionally connect with your audience.


  • Backlink Building:

    Create a network of high-quality backlinks from reputable websites to boost your website's authority and credibility. Focus on gaining backlinks through strategic guest blogging, influencer marketing, and social media engagement. These backlinks indicate to search engines that your website is a valuable and trustworthy source of information.


  • Analytics and Reporting:

    Use analytics tools and reports to continuously monitor and analyze your SEO performance. Determine areas for improvement, optimize strategies, and monitor the effectiveness of your SEO efforts. To determine the effectiveness of your strategies, evaluate keyword rankings, website traffic, and conversion rates on a regular basis. Utilize data-driven insights to guide your SEO journey.


  • SEO Tools and Expertise:

    Use SEO tools to gain valuable insights into website performance, track keyword rankings, and monitor backlink profiles. To gain data-driven insights into your SEO performance, use tools such as Google Search Console, Google Analytics, and Semrush. Consider working with an experienced SEO agency, such as WeDooSo, to get expert advice on developing and implementing effective SEO strategies.

    E-commerce businesses can lay a solid foundation for long-term success by approaching SEO holistically. Businesses can attract organic traffic, drive conversions, and achieve long-term growth in the competitive e-commerce landscape by optimizing website content, building backlinks, and utilizing effective analytics.

Which Ecommerce Platform is Best for SEO?

The platform you select for your online store can have a significant impact on your SEO efforts and, ultimately, your overall success. While there are numerous e-commerce platforms available, each with its own set of advantages and disadvantages, certain platforms stand out for their SEO friendliness and ability to propel you to the top of search engine results pages (SERPs).

Given the numerous options available, selecting an e-commerce platform can be a difficult decision. Each platform has its own set of features, functionalities, and pricing models. When it comes to SEO, however, certain factors stand out as critical considerations.

  • Built-in SEO Features:

    A seamless SEO optimization strategy is required for any e-commerce platform that wishes to dominate the digital landscape. Look for platforms that include SEO features like extensive meta tag optimization, granular control over URL structures, and automated sitemap generation. These features can greatly simplify your SEO process, saving you time and effort.


  • Performance and Speed: Websites that load quickly and efficiently are favored by search engines. Make sure that the platform you select prioritizes performance and speed optimization. This can help you in avoiding search engine penalties*, improving user experience, and increasing your ranking in SERPs.

    *A Google penalty means your site is either no longer listed on search results, or that your ranking for your targeted keywords has dropped dramatically. When your site gets a Google penalty, your target audience can't find you.


  • Content Management System (CMS): The CMS is critical to SEO effectiveness. Select a platform that allows for simple content creation, editing, and optimization. This will allow you to publish engaging and informative content that resonates with your target audience and attracts organic traffic on a consistent basis.


  • Third-Party Integrations: Consider platforms that seamlessly integrate with popular SEO tools and analytics platforms. These integrations provide valuable insights into your website's SEO performance, allowing you to make informed decisions based on data-driven evidence.


  • Community Support: A strong community of users and developers can be an invaluable resource for troubleshooting SEO issues, sharing knowledge, and optimizing your website. Platforms with a thriving community can provide the support and guidance you need to achieve SEO success.

Top Ecommerce Platforms for SEO

Based on the factors mentioned above, here are some of the top e-commerce platforms known for their SEO prowess:

  • WooCommerce:

    WooCommerce, a popular WordPress plugin, is known for its robust SEO features, which include extensive meta tag support, customizable URL structures, and automated sitemaps. It also integrates seamlessly with various SEO plugins and analytics tools, providing a comprehensive SEO toolkit.


  • BigCommerce:

    BigCommerce, a cloud-based e-commerce platform, provides a comprehensive set of SEO tools, such as advanced page speed optimization, XML sitemaps, and structured data markup support. It also seamlessly integrates with popular SEO analytics platforms, providing a comprehensive SEO solution.


  • Built-in SEO Features:

    A seamless SEO optimization strategy is required for any e-commerce platform that wishes to dominate the digital landscape. Look for platforms that include SEO features like extensive meta tag optimization, granular control over URL structures, and automated sitemap generation. These features can greatly simplify your SEO process, saving you time and effort.


  • Shopify:

    Another popular cloud-based e-commerce platform, Shopify, includes SEO-friendly features like meta tag optimization, URL structure control, and image alt text support. It also integrates with a variety of SEO plugins and tools, making it an easy-to-use solution for aspiring e-commerce entrepreneurs.


  • Magento:

    Magento, a powerful open-source e-commerce platform, includes advanced SEO features such as granular URL structure customization, multilingual support, and integration with various SEO plugins. However, managing and optimizing it requires more technical expertise.


  • Volusion

    Volusion, a cloud-based e-commerce platform, provides basic SEO features, such as meta tag optimization, URL structure control, and image alt text support. While its SEO capabilities may not be as comprehensive as some of the other platforms, it offers a straightforward and user-friendly approach to e-commerce.

Additional Considerations

In addition to the platform's built-in SEO features, consider your specific needs and preferences. If you have a large product catalog, Shopify or Magento may be better suited due to their scalability and advanced features. For smaller businesses or those with less technical expertise, WooCommerce or BigCommerce may be more manageable.
