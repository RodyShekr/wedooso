My name is Rody. I am the founder of WeDooSo, and I am happy to welcome you here. As a founder, I would like to provide an insight behind the scenes into how I came to found WeDooSo and what my passion is.

From a young age, I realized that I wanted to go my own way and become an entrepreneur. Even then, the business world, and especially the digital world, really appealed to me. I quickly realized that one day I would be part of it. Through my accumulated knowledge about digital marketing, I became familiar with how to run ads or optimize a website for entrepreneurial success in the 21st century. I knew I had to change something because I saw that many companies either don't have time, e.g., to effectively run advertising or optimize their websites, or do not have this knowledge at all. I was supposed to apply the knowledge I had learned to help other companies, and that is how WeDooSo finally came into being.
